Mongolian Experience project

This project is due to extraordinary landscape of Mongolia, undiscovered and unexplored culture. Situated between Siberian dense forests and Gobbi desert to the south, land-locked, it is far away from the main roads of civilization.

This made Mongolia isolated from exterier influences, made preservation of nomadic lifestyle of the most population. Modern winds made changes toward urbanization and industrialization, but still remains semi-nomadic character in the main cities.
